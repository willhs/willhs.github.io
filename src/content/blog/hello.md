This is the first post on the new willhs.me. It's less a blog and more a
working notebook — a place to publish writeups of the projects I build and
the ideas I chase on the side.

I've been a software engineer for about eight years, mostly full-stack web,
and I keep finding myself drawn to projects that don't fit anywhere: small
games, oddly-shaped tools, little experiments that are more interesting than
useful. Historically these have lived and died on my hard drive. The point
of this site is to change that habit.

## What to expect

- **Project writeups** — what I built, why, what broke, what I'd do next.
- **Notes from the middle of the thing**, not just the polished retrospective.
- **Short posts when short is enough.** Long ones when they earn it.

No newsletter, no comments, no brand. Just writing. If even one person finds
something here useful, that's the whole goal.

More soon.
